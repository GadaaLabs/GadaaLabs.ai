Large language models hallucinate — and they hallucinate most confidently when they know least. Retrieval-Augmented Generation solves this by grounding every response in documents retrieved from a controlled knowledge base. But RAG is not a single technique: it is a pipeline with at least six distinct engineering decisions, each of which can make or break the system's accuracy and latency. This course teaches every step, from chunking strategy to production deployment, with evaluation metrics at each stage so you know when you have actually improved things.
